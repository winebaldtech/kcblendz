Our Story

Born in a kitchen,
built for the world.

KCBlendz started with one rule: never compromise on the fruit. Born in our campus kitchen — Kitchen 2 Kongo, Pamplemousses, Mauritius — we've grown into a wellness brand serving customers across Mauritius, Nigeria, and shipping shelf-stable goods worldwide.

Our mission

Nourishing lives, inspiring wellness.

Wellness isn't a fad — it's daily nourishment, made simple. We exist to put real, whole fruit and plant-based wellness drinks within reach of every busy household, gym-goer, parent, and professional.

Every smoothie, juice, dried fruit and powder we make starts from raw ingredients sourced from local agro-processors. No syrups. No fillers. No shortcuts.

What we stand for

Our values

Real ingredients

Whole fruit, no concentrates or syrups. If you wouldn't eat it raw, we won't put it in.

Made daily

Fresh smoothies are blended that morning. Dried fruits and powders are batched weekly for peak freshness.

Local first

We partner with West African and Mauritian farmers and agro-processors. Buying KCBlendz feeds the local food economy.
