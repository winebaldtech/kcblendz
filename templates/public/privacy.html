KCBlendz (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — name, email, phone, delivery address, payment details — when you create an account, place an order, or contact us. We also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, and how you use our site, primarily for security and analytics.

How we use it

Sharing

We never sell your data. We share information with trusted service providers (payment processors like Paystack and PayPal, delivery partners, email and SMS providers) strictly to deliver our services. We may disclose information when required by law.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting hello@kcblendz.com. You can also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any of our messages.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your store region, hold your cart, and measure site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser, but parts of the site may not function correctly.

Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it.
